AHCI and SATA drives:
- SATA is the physical connector/connection, AHCI is the protocol. They are generally used on old optical disk and SSD.

PCIe and NVMe drives:
- PCIe is the physical connector/connection, NVMe is the procotol. They are generally used on recent SSD. PCIe is also used for a lot of others hardware that needs speed, as 
its directly connected to the CPU, for example GPU use it.
